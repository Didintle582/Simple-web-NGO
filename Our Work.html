Work carried out by Villa Heights

As Onkarabile day care our work is to make sure we do half of the parenting job that is needed
during the day, and make sure the children grow and become healthy and ready for the life of being in
primary school, so the children can build friendships with other children that they dont know.
our work is to take care of the children and being they second family and a place of fun and love for the
children.

We provide food, sleep, play area, games, lessons, and much more.
